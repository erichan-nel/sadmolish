Psychotheraphy
Psychtheraphy or "talk theraphy" is an effective treatment for clinical depression and is often the first form of treatment recommended for depression. During psychotherapy, you talk to a licensed and trained mental health professional who helps you work through depression in various ways. This approach teaches you the skills to deal with your depression and help prevent future occurences of depression as well. Therapy can be given in a variety of formats inncluding individual, group, couple, or family therapy. It is important to find a therapist that you can connect with and trust. In addition, there are several different approaches to therapy. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy, and Interpersonal Therapy have all been show to be effective for treating depression, altough effectiveness varies from person to person and other therapeutic approaches may work for you as well.

Antidepressants
Antidepressant treatment is also effective for treating clinical depressiono. It is more effective for severe cases of depression, and is commonly combined with psychotherapy for an even greater effect. There are many cases or antidepressants, such as SSRIs, SNRIs, NDRIs, or tricyclic antidepressants. Keep in mind that all of these drugs have various side effects and do not have an immediate effect, they take some time to work. If one antidepresant doesn't work well, you might try another drug of the same class of depression medicines altogether. Your doctor might also try changing the the dosage. In some cases, your doctor might recommended taking more than one medication for your depression.

Brain Stimulation
When medication and therapy fail to ease the symptomps of clinical depression, there are still additional effective options. Brain stimulation techniques can be used to treat major depression that has not responded to standard treatments. Electroconclusive theraphy (ECT) is the most well-known and proven type for brain stimulation, but there are also other approaches such as repetitive transcanial magnetic stimulation, deep brain stimulation, bagus nerve stimulation, and magnetic seizure therapy.
